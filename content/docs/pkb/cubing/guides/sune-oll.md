# SuneOLL

The notation describing the particular variation of Sune being used is as follows;

R = Right

L = Left

F = Front

B = Back

W = Wide

A = Anti

S = Sune


For example, RFS would be a regular Sune (RUR’URU2R’) while RBWAS would be the Right Back Wide AntiSune (r’U2RUR’Ur).
